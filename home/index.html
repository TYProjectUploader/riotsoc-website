Welcome to

UNSW Riot Games Society

We're a fun and welcoming environment for all students that are interested in or play any of Riot’s games. Whether you’re new, a casual player or a veteran, we’ll have an event for you!

Are you looking for awesome and friendly people to play with?


Our community is extremely active and welcoming, and our events are a great place to hang out and meet fellow Riot gamers. We run a range of casual and competitive events including trivia night, aram tournaments and our gaming weeklies!

Be sure to join our discord server for all the latest updates!
